Program Overview

Colorado Conventional Mortgage Loans

Conventional mortgages are the gold standard for qualified homebuyers who want the widest range of options. We leverage your strong credit, income, and assets to shop multiple wholesale lenders, structure PMI intelligently, and secure a loan that aligns with both your short-term budget and long-term wealth strategy.

Key Benefits

  • » PMI that can be removed once you reach the required equity threshold.
  • » Options for a wide range of property types, including second homes and rentals.
  • » Potentially lower overall cost of ownership compared to most government-backed loans.
  • » Competitive fixed and adjustable-rate structures tailored to your financial goals.

Who is this for?

This loan may be a great fit if you meet the following general criteria:

  • » Borrowers with strong credit scores (typically 620+; best pricing at 740+).
  • » Homebuyers with stable income, verifiable assets, and manageable debt-to-income ratios.
  • » Those purchasing or refinancing primary residences, second homes, or investment properties.
  • » Clients seeking jumbo-conforming or high-balance options above standard loan limits.

These are general guidelines. Contact us for a personalized assessment. Not a commitment to lend.

The Gold Standard in Home Financing

A Conventional Loan is the go-to for most qualified homebuyers, offering flexibility and long-term savings that government-backed loans can't match.

Conventional Loan

  • PMI Is Cancellable: PMI may be removed once you reach an equity level that meets investor or lender guidelines.
  • Flexible Property Types: Suitable for primary residences, second homes, and investment properties.
  • Potential for Lower Overall Cost: May offer a lower total cost over time depending on the borrower’s scenario.

FHA Loan (Government-Backed)

  • MIP for Life: Mortgage insurance typically lasts for the full loan term.
  • Primary Residence Only: Not eligible for second homes or investment properties.
  • Higher Insurance Cost: Includes both upfront and monthly premiums.

Why Conventional Beats FHA for Strong Borrowers

  • Lower Monthly Costs Over Time

    Remove PMI when equity reaches lender thresholds to reduce your payment.

  • Eligible for Second Homes & Investments

    Expand your real estate portfolio with the same familiar guidelines.

  • Rate & Term Flexibility

    Choose from fixed, ARM, and high-balance options to match your goals.
