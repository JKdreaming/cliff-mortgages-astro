Non-QM Loans in Colorado

When traditional underwriting doesn’t tell the full story, Non-QM loans open the door. We help self-employed borrowers, investors, and clients with recent credit events structure compelling scenarios using bank statements, assets, DSCR, and other alternative documentation.

Key Benefits

  • » Alternative Documentation Guidance: we outline what your lender needs and package it clearly.
  • » Vast Non-QM Lender Network: access specialty programs designed for unique borrower profiles.
  • » Creative Scenario Support: we tailor solutions when traditional guidelines fall short.
  • » Flexible structures for long-term rentals, short-term rentals, and complex income situations.

Who is this for?

This loan may be a great fit if you meet the following general criteria:

  • » Self-employed borrowers leveraging bank statements, P&Ls, or asset depletion.
  • » Real estate investors using DSCR or other cash-flow-based programs.
  • » Clients overcoming recent credit events, like bankruptcy or foreclosure.
  • » Foreign nationals or borrowers with complex, non-traditional income sources.

These are general guidelines. Contact us for a personalized assessment. Not a commitment to lend.

The Common-Sense Lending Advantage

Non-QM loans offer flexible, common-sense solutions when conventional financing falls short.

Non-QM Loan

  • Flexible Income Proof:
    Use bank statements, assets, or property cash flow to qualify—ideal for self-employed borrowers.
  • Overcome Credit Hurdles:
    Programs exist for borrowers with recent credit events like bankruptcy or foreclosure.
  • Investor Friendly:
    DSCR loans fall under the Non-QM umbrella, supporting rental portfolio growth.

Conventional Loan

  • Strict Income Rules:
    Requires W-2s and tax returns, which can be challenging for entrepreneurs.
  • Long Waiting Periods:
    Often forces multi-year waiting periods after major credit events.
  • Limited for Investors:
    Strict caps on financed properties can slow down portfolio expansion.
